Our History

Auto-Obsessions started simply, as most great things do , with a dad and his son on the sofa. They would regularly share their enthusiasm for cars, watching television , and making their own predictions as to which classic car would fetch the highest price..

Over time , this passion expanded to include many family members and friends , who were eager to put their automotive knowledge and reputations against one another.

Following his passion , Adam Glazer started developing an online version of their game, certain that he and his family were not alone in their excitement. Never having undertaken such a project, he pushed ahead, learning as he went along, His efforts culminated with a 1 day, and a 1,056 mile car trip to pitch the concept to interested automotive auction houses. Simply explained as "Fantasy Footbal for Gear Heads" his passion was shared, and Auto-Obsessions was launched July 23,2014.
